Tours

A Tour of Duty in Mann vs. Machine (MvM) is a series of missions requiring a Tour of Duty Ticket or Squad Surplus Vouchers. Completing missions earns Operation badges, tracking progress through the tour. Tours offer specific rewards, including Botkiller weapons and Australium weapons for advanced tours, like Operation Two Cities.

Operation Oil Spill

Rewards

Rust Botkiller weapons Mk.I / Blood Botkiller weapons Mk.I

Operation Steel Trap

Rewards

Silver Botkiller weapons Mk.I / Gold Botkiller weapons Mk.I / Australium weapons

Operation Mecha Engine

Rewards

Silver Botkiller weapons Mk.II / Gold Botkiller weapons Mk.II / Australium weapons

Operation Twin Cities

Rewards

Killstreak Kit Fabricator / Australium weapons

Operation Gear Grinder

Rewards

Carbonado Botkiller weapons Mk.I / Diamond Botkiller weapons Mk.I / Australium weapons
